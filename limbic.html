Memories of Mars & Tropico 6

Throughout my career I worked with various AAA companies, Limbic Entertainment is one of the best from my experience.

It is a medium sized company based in south of Frankfurt am Main, they are the creative minds behind Memories of mars, Might and Magic Heroes VII and Tropico series as well as experts in Unreal Engine 4 and console platform development.

A few things that I did...

  • In charge of a task force made up of three people that focuses on core gameplay features for AAA games.
  • Refactoring old systems and maximizing their efficiency to ensure a desired stable framerate across all platforms (PC/PS4/XBOX).
  • Architected a Network Traffic encryption system in UE4 and a Key Distribution Web Service using GOLang and AWS Lambda.
