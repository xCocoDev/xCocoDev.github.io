Numa

My first demo game project made with Unity. Numa is a story based game that takes place in the future where advanced AI's conquered our planet. The player's main objective is to find out the truth about itself after reaches the end of the game more exactly how it was involved in the AI revolution and take a final decision.

Trailer

Interesting things that I did

  • AI Behavior with states such as: patrolling and fleeing.
  • Player and Camera movement controllers.
  • Achievement, Inventory and Checkpoint system.
  • In-game UI systems such as the actionbar and inventory one.
  • Player Interaction systems with the environment objects (collectables, sounds, particle effects).
