World of Warcraft

One of the biggest and most known MMORPG in the game industry. Also the reason why I started my game development career at just 13 years old (2011) when I decided that I want to build my own World of Warcraft server (quite ambitious).

As of that moment for the next two years I spent my spare time in learning and extending the TrinityCore C++ framework . Right after that I got hired as a freelancer for multiple WoW servers around the world as my programming skills started to grow.

Three years later I end up working full-time for the biggest World of Warcraft private servers in the world where I have been working ever since (until April 2019).

During those years I had the chance to work with top industry professionals that are working in AAA studios and a huge code base and learn a lot from this experience.

Interesting things that I did

  • Scripted over 60 complex raids/dungeons boss fights that lasts more than 10 minutes that benefited the server by gaining 2000 more players to its daily player base.
  • Implemented a multi-threaded system for crowded zones/areas splitting them in multiple smaller sections that increased overall server performance by 10%-12%.
  • Developed a battleground cross faction system that reduced the overall time of waiting in queue by 45% where players from opposite faction can join each others team.
  • Implemented a brawl system, an extended version of the traditional battleground, that features power-ups and additional mechanics.
  • Architected a building system where all the players on the server could contribute to the constructions rewarding them with all kind of in-game goods upon completion.
  • Developed an artifact trait system where players can upgrade their artifact weapons (up to four) by using in-game currency and getting new character abilities or improving the current ones.
  • Implemented Mythic+ Dungeon Keystone system that offers players an endlessly scaling challenge, where team of five people compete against a timer.
  • Scripted classes mechanics such as: spells, auras, talents and items.
