They Can Hear You

A stealth sniper game made with Unity. The scene takes place in a radioactive wasteland. The character main objective is to stealthily cross the radioactive city to the extraction zone either by silently killing the monsters or distracting them with shot sounds or other interactables found in the environment.

Footage

Interesting things that I did

  • AI Behavior with a complex Finite State Machine, it features states such as: patrolling, chasing, evading, investigating, melee/range combat.
  • AI system to track which enemies are in combat or distracted by the player.
  • AI hearing sensor that can detect player's footsteps, gun shots in a semi-realistic manner using occlusion.
  • AI system where an Enemy can alert his nearby allies when he sees the player.
  • Audio system to adjust the sounds to be more immersive based on player current state whether is in combat with enemies or was recently seen by one.
  • Loot and ammo system.
  • UI indicator for AI's that fills up and changes color based on their awareness level (Attacking, Chasing, Investigating, Idle).
  • Player Interaction systems with the environment objects (collectables, sounds, particle effects).
